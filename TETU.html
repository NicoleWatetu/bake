* Tetus Treats Bakery *

Tetus Treats all the way for all your sweet cravings! With the sole purpose of introducing people to a variety of quality cakes, we are the pioneers of FRESH CREAM cakes and THE ONLY HALAL bakery that boasts the biggest variety of cakes in Kenya. Established in 2012 with our flagship branch located in Westlands, next to Westlands Banquet Center & Soin Arcade, elaborately structured in the form of an attractive Strawberry Cake dripping with chocolate, we have grown rapidly with a total of 11 branches Nationwide and have grasped a large chunk of the cake lovers market. We aim to offer our high quality products at a competitive price to meet the demands of the middle-to higher-income, local and expatriate market, in homes and offices.
